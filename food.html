DELICIOUS SOUTH KOREAN CUISINE

Kimchi

A traditional fermented Korean side dish made of seasoned vegetables.

Bibimbap

A mixed rice dish topped with vegetables, meat, and a spicy gochujang sauce.

Bulgogi

Marinated and grilled beef, typically served with rice or wrapped in lettuce leaves.

Tteokbokki

Spicy and sweet rice cakes, a popular street food in South Korea.

Samgyeopsal

Grilled pork belly, often served with garlic, onions, and dipping sauces.

Sundubu Jjigae

A hot and spicy stew featuring uncurdled tofu, vegetables, sometimes meat, and gochujang (chili paste).

Japchae

A savory and slightly sweet dish of stir-fried glass noodles and vegetables.

Jeon
